---
title: Commmands
description: Learn about the commands available in ItsMyConvert, a powerful tool that allows you to convert schematics and files to different formats with ease.
---

- `schem <schematic> <current-version> <to-version> [to-versions]`<br/>
  Converts a schematic from one version to another or multiple versions.
- `world <schematic> <current-version> <to-version> [to-versions]`<br/>
  Converts a schematic to a world from one version to another or multiple versions.
- `testblock <block> <from-version> <to-version>`<br/>
  Tests a block conversion from one version to another.
- `reload`<br/>
  Reloads the configuration.
- `end`<br/>
  Quits the program.

ItsMyBot is a self-hosted modular Discord bot. Developed with TypeScript and Node.js, it is designed to be easily modifiable through an addon system. The core of the bot has limited functionalities to allow room for addons. It is frequently updated to enhance its features and fix bugs. Feel free to join our [Discord server](https://itsmy.studio/discord) to share your suggestions and report any issues.

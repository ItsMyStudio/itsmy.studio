This addon allows you to track invites of users. You can see how many invites a user has, who invited them, and more. You can also add bonus invites to users, which can be useful for rewarding users for inviting others to the server.

[Purchase](https://builtbybit.com/resources/51993/)


## Commands
- `/invites [user]`<br/>
  Shows the invites of the user. If no user is specified, it will show your invites.
- `/inviter <user>`<br/>
  Shows the inviter of the user.
- `/invite add-bonus <user> <amount>`<br/>
  Adds bonus invites to the user.
- `/invite remove-bonus <user> <amount>`<br/>
  Removes bonus invites from the user.
- `/invite set-bonus <user> <amount>`<br/>
  Sets the bonus invites of the user.
- `/leaderboard invites`<br/>
  Shows the invites leaderboard.

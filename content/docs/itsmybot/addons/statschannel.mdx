title: StatsChannel

---

Display the placeholders of your choice in a channel, taking advantage of over 50 available ItsMyBot placeholders. This plugin allows you to show placeholders in statistics channels, updating them every 5 minutes to modify the member count, the status of your Minecraft server, and more.

[Download](https://builtbybit.com/resources/29520/)

## Commands
- `/statschannel create <name>`<br/>
  Create a stats channel with the given name.
- `/statschannel rename <channel> <name>`<br/>
  Rename the stats channel.

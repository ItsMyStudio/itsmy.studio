The moderation addon are designed to help you moderate your server. They include a variety of features to help you manage your server effectively.

[Purchase](https://builtbybit.com/resources/51994/)

## Commands
- `/logs <user>`<br/>
  Show the logs of a user.
- `/clear <amount> [channel] [user]`<br/>
  Clear messages in a channel.
- `/warn <user> <reason> [silent]`<br/>
  Warn a user.
- `/tempban <user> <duration> [reason]`<br/>
  Temporarily ban a user.
